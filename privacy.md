# Privacy Policy — Lil Blunt: The Smoke Realm

_Last updated: 2026-07-19 · Operator: SmokeRing ("we"). Contact:
support@smokering.game (once live; until then, the itch.io page)._

## What we collect, and only if you opt in

| Data | When | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Email + optional display name | ONLY if you tick the consent box on the optional signup panel | Weekly rank report, tips, milestone emails. Double opt-in: campaign email starts only after you click the confirm link. |
| Wallet address | ONLY if you press CONNECT WALLET | Leaderboard identity (shown truncated), token-holder perks, badge mint. Wallet addresses are already public on-chain; we never see keys and never initiate transactions without your wallet's confirmation screen. |
| Pseudonymous gameplay events | During play, tied to a random ID stored on your device (never your name/email) | Adaptive difficulty, leaderboards, improving the game. Auto-deleted after 90 days. |
| Anonymous button clicks (event name only) | Menu/email interactions | Understanding which features matter. No profiles, no cross-site tracking, no ad networks. |

We do NOT collect: names (beyond an optional handle), passwords, payment
data, precise location, contacts, or device identifiers. We never sell data.

## Your rights (GDPR/POPIA/CCPA-style, honored for everyone)

- **Export your data**: open the link in any email footer →
  `/data-export?token=…` returns everything stored about you as JSON.
- **Delete your account data** (right to be forgotten):
  `/data-delete?token=…` (also linked in every email footer) erases your
  email record and gameplay stats immediately. In-game data on YOUR device
  (saves) is yours; delete it by clearing the game's browser storage.
- **Unsubscribe**: one click in any email, instant, no login.

No email account? Your gameplay data is already pseudonymous (random ID);
clear your browser storage and the ID link is severed permanently.

## Where data lives

Cloudflare Workers KV (encrypted at rest), AgentMail (email delivery),
itch.io (game hosting — see their policies for what the page itself logs).
Support emails you send us are processed by an AI assistant (Mistral/Kimi)
to draft replies; flagged threads are read by a human.

## Children

The game is not directed at children under 13; do not opt into email or
connect a wallet if you are under the age of digital consent where you live.
